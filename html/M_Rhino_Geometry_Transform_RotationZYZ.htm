A transform matrix from Euler angles.
Remarks
RotationZYZ(alpha, beta, gamma) = R_z(alpha) * R_y(beta) * R_z(gamma) where R_*(angle) is rotation of angle radians about the corresponding *-world coordinate axis. Note, alpha and gamma are in the range (-pi, pi] while beta in in the range [0, pi]
